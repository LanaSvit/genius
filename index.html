Artist statement

My art is a way to discover my self, to express the inner emptiness
and to share my vision of beauty with the world.
A collage technique by itself for me is the embodiment
of the principle of freedom, where I can dive into the unknown,
to shed a different light on things and playfully create new worlds
from those that have outlived themselves.
